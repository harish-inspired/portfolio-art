The Artist

Every Line Tells
a Story

I am Michael — pencil art isn't just what I do. It's how I see the world.

For over a decade, I've been translating moments, memories, and emotions into graphite. Each commission begins the same way: a conversation. I want to understand not just what you want drawn, but what you want to feel when you hold it in your hands.

My work spans portraits that capture the exact warmth of a smile, life drawings that freeze a fleeting moment of grace, and landscapes that carry the weight and stillness of a place you love. No two pieces are ever the same — and that's the whole point.

Based locally and shipping internationally, I work with clients across the world. Whether you're around the corner or on the other side of the globe, your commission receives the same personal attention, the same obsessive care, and the same commitment to getting it exactly right.

How to Commission
Your Piece

Simple, personal, and collaborative from start to delivery.

01
💬

Share Your Vision

Tell me what you'd like — who or what you want drawn, the mood, the size. Send a reference photo if you have one. No idea too big or too small.

02
📋

Receive a Custom Quote

Within 24–48 hours I'll send a personalised quote based on the size, complexity, and type of artwork. No obligation, no pressure.

03
✏️

Once confirmed, I get to work. You'll receive regular progress updates so you can see your artwork coming to life, stroke by stroke.
